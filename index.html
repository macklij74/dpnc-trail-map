Trail Tips

Stay safe & have fun on every hike

🧢 Before You Go
👟
Wear sturdy shoes or hiking boots
Trails can be muddy and rooted after rain
💧
Bring plenty of water
At least 16 oz per person per mile
🧴
Apply sunscreen & bug spray
Ticks are active spring through fall — check afterward!
📱
Download this app for offline use
Cell service is spotty in deep woods
🐕 Trail Rules (Official)
🐕
Leashed pets permitted on trails west of Pequotsepos Road
Dogs must be on leash at all times — pick up after them!
🚯
Pack it in, pack it out
Leave No Trace — take all trash with you
📍
Stay on marked trails
Protects ground-nesting animals and fragile native plants
🚲
Bikes on designated paths only
See the trail map for the designated bicycle route
🌿
No collecting or disturbing plants and animals
Enjoy them — but leave everything as you found it
🔥
No smoking, fires, camping or hunting
These are strictly prohibited on all DPNC properties
🚨 Safety
🌩️
Check weather before heading out
Lightning? Head to the Nature Center building
📞
Emergency: call 911
DPNC office: (860) 536-1216
👨‍👧
Kids? Try the Laurel Loop first
Flat, short, and packed with cool stuff to find
